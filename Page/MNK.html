Job Gauge

Chakra Gauge

When you learn the action Meditation, the Chakra Gauge will be displayed.
Accumulating the maximum amount of Chakra will allow you to execute several actions such as Steel Peak, Howling Fist (acquired at level 40), The Forbidden Chakra (acquired at level 54), and Enlightenment (acquired at level 74).

Master's Gauge

Upon learning the trait Enhanced Perfect Balance, you will unlock the Master's Gauge as well as Solar and Lunar Nadi. You can open Chakra by using a weaponskill while Perfect Balance is in effect. There are three types of Chakra: Coeurl Chakra, Raptor Chakra, and Opo-opo Chakra.
When three of the same type of Chakra have been opened, you can use the action Elixir Field.
When one of each type of Chakra has been opened, you can use the action Flint Strike.
Using Elixir Field will open the Lunar Nadi, while using Flint Strike will open the Solar Nadi. You can use Tornado Kick by opening three Chakra regardless of type.
